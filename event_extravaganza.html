Special Event

Coffee Tasting Extravaganza

Explore flavor notes, brewing methods, and origin stories in one immersive evening.

← Back to Events
November 15, 2026 6:00 PM

Join us for our Community Coffee Meet-Up, where coffee lovers can gather to share their passion for all things caffeinated. This informal event encourages networking and connection among fellow coffee aficionados. Enjoy a cup of our finest brews, participate in engaging discussions, and even bring your favorite coffee-related stories or tips to share. Whether you’re a seasoned coffee drinker or just starting your journey, everyone is welcome!

Guided by our barista team, you will compare aroma, body, and acidity across multiple brews, learn simple testing techniques, and pick up tips you can use at home.

Whether you are just starting your coffee journey or already a dedicated enthusiast, this evening is designed to be relaxed, interactive, and delicious.
